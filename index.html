Services

Location

In most cases, photoshoots take place at our studio. We understand that artwork size or site-specific needs may require an on-location photoshoot. We will work together to identify the location that will yield the best results.

Equipment & Process

  • Nikon Z 7 45.7 megapixel camera and Nikon S-Line lenses capture exceptional detail.
  • Professional studio strobe lighting with filters eliminates glare from glossy surfaces. This is particularly effective for oil paintings or works covered with resin.
  • X-Rite® color management target and software ensures accurate and consistent color.
  • Tethered studio setup provides immediate photo previews within Adobe Lightroom.

Considerations

Depending on the quantity of artwork being photographed, clients are welcome to be present during the photoshoot.

Photoshoot Guidelines

When preparing for your session, please note the following items:

  • Frames, 2-D artwork should be unframed, unless the frame is integral to the piece or needs to be documented for selling purposes. Frames can cause unwanted shadows and reflected color that impact the accuracy of the image.
  • Glass or Plexiglass, 2-D artwork that is behind glass or plexiglass should be removed to prevent unwanted reflections and glare.
  • Artwork size, the maximum artwork dimensions for photoshoots at our studio is 48 x 48 inches. Size restrictions for sessions at an artist's studio will be based on available space for lighting and camera equipment.
