Wenc Photo is a Chicago-based service, specializing in the photography of artwork for artists and galleries.

In today's digitally-driven art world, having accurate, high-quality images of your artwork is essential for artists to successfully represent themselves to collectors, galleries, jurors, artist grants, MFA programs, and to the public via portfolio websites and social media. In addition, the images we capture are ideal for print reproduction.

We're dedicated to fulfilling these photographic needs and look forward to collaborating with you.

Services

Our studio or on location

Artists have the option to schedule photoshoots at our studio or theirs. When scheduling we can determine which location will work best for the client and yield optimum results.

Equipment & Process

  • Nikon Z 7 45.7 megapixel camera with excellent detail capture used with exceptionally sharp Nikon S-Line lenses.
  • Professional studio strobe lighting with filters to eliminate glare from glossy surfaces, particulary effective when photographing oil paintings or works covered with resin.
  • X-Rite color management target and software is used to capture accurate, consistent colors.
  • Teathered studio setup provides the client immediate previews of photos within Adobe Lightroom.

Collaborate

The artist or gallerist should be present during the photoshoot to proof images. Please allow approximately 20 minutes per artwork plus additional setup time for artist studio sessions. The timing per piece may vary depending on the artwork media. These factors will be discussed during the scheduling process.

See get started section for additional photoshoot guidelines.

Photoshoot Guidelines

When preparing for your session, please note the following items:

  • Frames, 2-D artwork should be unframed, unless the frame is integral to the piece or needs to be documented for selling purposes. Frames can cause unwanted shadows and reflected color that impact the accuracy of the image.
  • Glass or Plexiglass, 2-D artwork that is behind glass or plexiglass should be removed to prevent unwanted reflections and glare.
  • Artwork size, the maximum artwork dimensions for photoshoots at our studio is 48 x 48 inches. Size restrictions for sessions at an artist's studio will be based on available space for lighting and camera equipment.
