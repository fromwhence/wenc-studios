Wenc Photo is a Chicago-based service, specializing in the photography of artwork for artists and galleries.

In today's digitally-driven art world, having accurate, high-quality images of your artwork is essential for artists to successfully represent themselves to collectors, galleries, jurors, MFA programs, and to the public via social media and portfolio websites.

We're dedicated to fulfilling these photographic needs and look forward to collaborating with you. In addition, the images we capture are ideal for print reproduction and archival purposes.

What we do

Our studio or on location

Artists have the option to schedule photoshoots at our studio or theirs. When scheduling we can determine which location will work best for the client and yield optimum results.

Equipment & Process

  • Nikon Z 7 45.7 megapixel camera with exceptionally sharp Nikon Z prime lenses.
  • Professional studio lighting with filters to eliminate glare from glossy surfaces, particulary effective when photographing oil paintings.
  • Color management target and software is used to capture accurate colors.
  • Teathered studio setup provides the client immediate previews of photos within Adobe Lightroom.

Collaborate

The artist or gallerist should be present during the photoshoot to proof images. Please allow approximately 20 minutes per artwork plus additional setup time for artist studio sessions. The timing per piece may vary depending on the artwork media. These factors will be discussed during the scheduling process.

See scheduling section for additional photoshoot guidelines.

Deliverables

Edited files are available to the client within 48 hours of the photoshoot and can be accessed using Dropbox. See the rates section for file type specifications.

Rates

We provide three tiers of service to best meet our clients diverse needs. All options include the delivery of your artwork in following file formats with associated color space.

Additional file formats are available upon request.

  • High resolution TIFF (ProPhoto RGB)
  • High resolution JPG (sRGB)
  • Web friendly JPG (sRGB)

Additional file formats are available upon request.


Tier 1

  • $25 per artwork
  • 2-D artwork is cropped to remove the background.
  • 3-D artwork is documented from one direction.
  • 3 files received for each artwork.

Tier 2

  • $30 per artwork
  • Client receives images from Tier 1 plus the following version:
  • 2-D artwork on white background, with or without a drop shadow. This works especially well for artwork with varied edges.
  • 3-D artwork is documented from additional direction.
  • 6 files received for each artwork.

Tier 3

  • $35 per artwork
  • For 2-D artwork only
  • Client receives images from Tier 1 and Tier 2, plus an image documenting the artwork from the side.
  • 9 files received for each artwork.

Payment

Following client approval of images, an invoice will be provided for all services rendered. State and local sales taxes are added to the net total. After payment is received, all imagess will be available for download on Dropbox.

Payment Methods

All major credit cards, Cash App, and Venmo.

Additional Fees

For in artist studio sessions, there is a three artwork minimum and a $50 fee to cover travel and equipment set-up time.

For travel greater than 25 miles, an additional $2.00/mile charge is applied to artist studio session fee.
